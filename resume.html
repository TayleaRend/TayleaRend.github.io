The Bue House OOSH

Working at The Blue House OOSH service is one of the most rewarding experiences I’ve had. Every day is different full of energy, creativity, and connection. We get to create a safe and fun space for children to learn, play, and grow outside of school hours. What I love most about OOSH work is the relationships we build not just with the kids, but also with families and schools. We’re often the first people children see after a long school day, and it’s our job to make them feel welcome, supported, and valued. Whether it’s helping with homework, setting up activities, or just listening when they want to talk, we play a big role in their day-to-day lives. The job also requires flexibility and patience. Every child is different some are full of energy and ready to play, while others need a bit of quiet time to recharge. It’s about reading the room, adapting to different needs, and creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels they belong. Working in OOSH also means being organised and responsible from planning engaging programs to ensuring safety and supervision at all times. Behind the fun and games is a lot of teamwork, communication, and care. Overall, working in OOSH isn’t just a job it’s about making a positive difference in children’s lives every single day. It’s about helping them build confidence, friendships, and memories that last well beyond the school years.
